Frequently Asked Questions

Your questions, answered

What is GreenVolt-Nexus?

GreenVolt-Nexus provides solar-powered EV charging stations in Jaipur , using non-exhaustible energy sourcces.

How do I charge my vehicle?

Locate a station via our map, plug in your EV, and pay securely through your ease of payment mode.

Are the stations operational 24/7?

Most stations operate from 9:00 AM to 10:00 PM. Check specific hours on our Schedule page.

Is it eco-friendly?

Yes, all our stations use solar energy, reducing carbon emissions and promoting sustainability.
